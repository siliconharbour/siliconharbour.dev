# Career Pages & ATS Research: Companies A-M

Research date: 2026-04-24

## Summary

Researched 54 companies from A-M that don't currently have job import sources configured.
Key findings:
- **2 companies with supported ATS**: CGI (njoyn/other), GroundControl (YCombinator jobs)
- **~10 companies with custom careers pages** worth building scrapers for
- **Most companies are small** (1-10 employees) with no formal careers pages

## Legend

- **Priority**: High = has active job postings on scrapeable page; Medium = has careers page but rarely posts; Low = no careers page or too small

## Actionable Findings

### High Priority — Worth Building Scrapers

1. **CGI Inc.** — Uses **Njoyn** ATS at `cgi.njoyn.com`. Large employer with regular NL postings including St. John's and Halifax. Custom scraper needed for Njoyn platform.
2. **GroundControl** — Posts on **YCombinator's Work at a Startup** job board. 2 active positions for St. John's. Could scrape the YC company jobs page.

### Medium Priority — Monitor / Custom Scraper Candidates

3. **Fortis Inc.** — Custom careers page at `fortisinc.com/careers`. Posts infrequently at HQ but is a large employer. Also uses CareerBeacon for some postings.
4. **AltoMaxx Technologies** — Custom careers page at `altomaxx.com/careers/`. Has 2-4 active postings. Email-based applications.
5. **Compactica** — Careers page at `compactica.com/careers`. Fast-growing startup (13 people). Open hiring via email but no specific listings.
6. **ACENET** — Simple careers page at `ace-net.ca/careers.html`. Occasional postings.
7. **GRi Simulations** — "Apply for a job" on their site. Active LinkedIn hiring.
8. **JAC Digital** — Posts jobs on their news page and CareerBeacon. Could track via CareerBeacon scraper.

### CareerBeacon Postings Detected

Several companies post on **CareerBeacon** rather than their own sites:
- Fortis Inc.
- JAC Digital
- Various other NL companies

Since CareerBeacon is already a supported ATS type, jobs from these companies may already be capturable through a CareerBeacon employer feed if one exists.

### Not Worth Pursuing (Too Small / No Careers Infrastructure)

The remaining ~40 companies are very small (1-10 employees), have no careers pages, and hire through direct outreach, LinkedIn, or email. Building scrapers for these would have minimal return.
